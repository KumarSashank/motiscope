Questions

The parts people ask about.

Does this replace my animation library?+

No. Cadence produces the numbers; your library plays them. It emits a curve and a duration, and GSAP, CSS or Framer does the rest.

What about scroll-driven motion?+

Scroll has no clock. Cadence reports ratios — how far one layer travels per pixel of scroll — and refuses to pretend those are seconds.

Where does it get the timing?+

From the pixels. A dense motion-energy curve gives duration, beat boundaries and the easing shape; the rest is read off a handful of frames.
